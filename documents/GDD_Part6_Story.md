## 6.1 NARRATIVE OVERVIEW

### Setting

**World:** Yaqar (from Hebrew יקר "yekar" meaning "precious")  
**Time Period:** Late 1700s to early 1800s equivalent (fantasy)  
**Primary Location:** Higueras (fictional country inspired by Honduras)  
**Secondary Locations:** Countries inspired by France, Saudi Arabia, Ethiopia, Thailand, Japan

### Central Conflict

A **global water crisis** threatens Yaqar. The **Godai** — five elemental serpent deities (Space, Fire, Ice, Wind, Stone) — have been imprisoned or scattered. **Lien**, the young Water Godai, must be returned to her temple to restore the world's water.

Two factions seek to capture Lien for their own ends:
- **Demons** led by **Lord Tempura** — seek to control the water supply and rule Yaqar
- **Angels** led by the **Archangels** — believe they should control the Godai, not mortals

**Helio** (a dragon) and **Anigi** (the Dragon Priestess) rescue Lien and embark on a journey across Yaqar to return her home while helping reunite the other Godai.

### Tone

- **Dark fantasy adventure** with moments of humor and heart
- **Complex factions** — neither angels nor demons are purely good or evil
- **Found family** — Helio, Anigi, and Lien form a bond through their journey
- **Themes:** Faith, leadership, found family, colonialism, nature vs. industry

## 6.2 MAIN CHARACTERS

### Helio (Player 1)

**Role:** Main playable character  
**Species:** Balloon Dragon (maintains child-like form despite being an adult)  
**Visual:** Blue spherical dragon, small wings, expressive eyes  
**In-Game Sprite:** Blue ball (current prototype)

**Personality:**
- Loves treasure, fighting, and exploring
- Aloof toward most people, deeply loyal to Anigi
- Personality inspired by "Bully Maguire" meme — cocky, confident, slightly antagonistic
- Slowly learning to care about others beyond his small circle

**Abilities:**
- Can use Gems to transform into other Dragon species
- This ability is limited during the game due to Gem scarcity (Tempura's forces mine them)
- Instead, Lien uses her Angelcraft to transform Helio

**Backstory:**
- Origin is mysterious (hints at Demon heritage, not revealed in this game)
- Lives as a nomad with Anigi in the Higueran rainforest
- Attends Anigi's Dragon church and considers her his best friend

### Anigi (Player 2 / Commander in Tactical Mode)

**Role:** Co-protagonist, Dragon Priestess, tactical commander  
**Species:** Human (Furiga heritage)  
**Visual:** Young woman, practical clothing, can make clothes from anything  
**In-Game Sprite:** Purple ball (current prototype)

**Personality:**
- Ambitious — dreams of ruling Higueras as Queen to unite its people
- Compassionate toward Dragons, often at odds with human authorities
- Confident leader thanks to training from her uncle-figure Máximo
- Loves fashion and is creative with materials

**Abilities:**
- Gemcraft (Fire and Space) — crystals grow inside her and must be used regularly
- If she doesn't use her Gemcraft, crystals will overtake her body (like her mother and brother)
- Commands dragon armies in tactical battles

**Backstory:**
- Captured by cultists as a child, imbued with Fire and Space Crystals
- Lost her father Arünei after an attack on Fort Omoa
- Raised partly by Mayor Máximo, who trained her in leadership and strategy
- Founded a Dragon church that serves underprivileged Dragons

### Lien (Escort Character / Light Ball)

**Role:** The key to restoring the world's water  
**Species:** Godai (Water elemental serpent, half-Angel due to heritage)  
**Visual:** Small blue light ball floating around Player 1  
**Full Form:** Water serpent (seen in cutscenes)

**Personality:**
- Eccentric, energetic, vibrant — inspired by Sisu from Raya and the Last Dragon
- Naive and friendly, sometimes too trusting
- Colorful speech patterns, enthusiastic about everything

**Backstory:**
- Conceived by the Fire Godai (Nobu/Quadamr) and Ice Godai (Mìyuè/Penemue)
- Raised by all four elemental Godai
- Ran away when they refused to help stop Lord Tempura's return
- Captured by Tempura's forces while trying to fight alone
- Rescued by Helio and Anigi at the start of the game

**Abilities:**
- Angelcraft (due to half-Angel nature) — uses this to transform Helio since Gems are scarce
- Connection to water — can sense the Godai and guide the party to them
- Will eventually become leader of the Godai

## 6.3 THE GODAI (ELEMENTAL SERPENTS)

The Godai are elemental deities, each consisting of an **Angel** and **Dragon** pair that merge to form one being.

### Lien — Water Godai
- **Angel:** N/A (Lien is unique — half-Angel and half-Dragon herself)
- **Dragon:** Lien's dragon form
- **Status:** Free but weakened, traveling with Helio and Anigi
- **Goal:** Return to her temple to restore global water

### Ka-Hi — Fire Godai
- **Angel:** Nobu (intense, passionate, fierce sense of justice, Lien's father)
- **Dragon:** Quadamr (mild-mannered, calm)
- **Status:** Imprisoned
- **Region:** Volcanic region (World 4)

### Hyōri — Ice Godai
- **Angel:** Mìyuè (poised, serene, smokes when alone, Lien's mother)
- **Dragon:** Penemue (compassionate, affectionate, coils around loved ones)
- **Status:** Imprisoned
- **Region:** Frozen region (World 5)

### Fūze — Wind Godai
- **Angel:** Langit (whimsical, restless, talks fast, repeats words)
- **Dragon:** Ariel (stable, deliberate, loves freedom)
- **Status:** Imprisoned
- **Region:** Sky region (World 6)

### Chi-Tsu — Stone Godai
- **Angel:** Dharan (stoic, patient, rarely speaks, protective)
- **Dragon:** Quagon (talkative, surprisingly enthusiastic)
- **Status:** Imprisoned
- **Region:** Mountain/underground region (World 7)

## 6.4 ANTAGONISTS

### Lord Tempura (Primary Antagonist)

**Role:** Demon Lord seeking to control Yaqar through the water supply  
**Origin:** Shokokai, fictional country inspired by Japan  
**Visual:** Imposing demonic figure, traditional Japanese-inspired armor

**Motivation:**
- Control Lien → Control water → Control Yaqar
- Has rebuilt his army after previous defeat
- His forces mine Gems and Crystals, causing the scarcity

**Forces:**
- Demon armies of various Sin classes (Pride, Wrath, Greed, Gluttony, Sloth, Lust, Envy)
- Human collaborators and cultists
- Wrath Demon mercenaries (Gaunt, Grapple, Happyday)

### The Archangels (Secondary Antagonists)

**Role:** Angel faction that believes they should control the Godai  
**Motivation:** Believe mortals (humans and dragons) are unfit to interact with divine beings

**Notable Members:**
- Various Archangels commanding angel forces
- Ruiners (inverse of Archangels) serve as elite units

**Philosophy:**
- Not "evil" in traditional sense — believe they're protecting cosmic order
- View Helio and Anigi as dangerous for associating with Lien
- Will pursue the party throughout the journey

### Demon Mercenary Trio

**Gaunt and Grapple** — Demon merchants/mercenaries who ride **Happyday** (Wrath Demon blimp)

- Initially trick Lien in the opening
- Recurring antagonists throughout the game
- After main story: attempt to sell pieces of a destroyed Godai, become final bosses

## 6.6 WORLD STRUCTURE (REVISED)

The nine worlds are restructured around the journey to help the Godai and return Lien home.

### World 1: Higueran Rainforest
**Theme:** Tutorial, introduction to Helio and Anigi's home  
**Gimmick:** Basic platforming, Lien rescue  
**Story:** Helio and Anigi rescue Lien from Tempura's forces, begin journey  
**Boss:** Demon commander (tutorial boss)

### World 2: Crystal Caves (Minos Mountains)
**Theme:** Underground, gem mines occupied by demons  
**Gimmick:** Bouncy crystals, darkness, mining hazards  
**Story:** Discover Tempura's mining operation depleting Gems  
**Boss:** Mine overseer demon

### World 3: Seaside Cliffs (Caxine Bay)
**Theme:** Ocean, Mayor Máximo's territory  
**Gimmick:** Water currents, tides, naval elements  
**Story:** Reunite with Máximo, learn about Godai locations  
**Boss:** Angel naval commander

### World 4: Volcanic Ruins — Fire Godai Region
**Theme:** Lava, ancient temples, fire hazards  
**Gimmick:** Sinking platforms, lava geysers  
**Story:** Free Ka-Hi (Fire Godai — Nobu and Quadamr)  
**Boss:** Fire demon warden → Ka-Hi (friendly after victory)

### World 5: Frozen Peaks — Ice Godai Region
**Theme:** Ice, snow, Hyōri's domain  
**Gimmick:** Slippery surfaces, ice puzzles  
**Story:** Free Hyōri (Ice Godai — Mìyuè and Penemue), Lien reunites with parents  
**Boss:** Ice angel warden → Hyōri (friendly after victory)

### World 6: Sky Citadel — Wind Godai Region
**Theme:** Floating islands, clouds, ancient Grouch temple  
**Gimmick:** Wind gusts, cloud platforms  
**Story:** Free Fūze (Wind Godai — Langit and Ariel), confront Quetzal's influence  
**Boss:** Wind demon warden → Fūze (friendly after victory)

### World 7: Lalibos Highlands — Stone Godai Region
**Theme:** Ethiopian-inspired highlands, mountains, caves  
**Gimmick:** Earthquake hazards, falling rocks  
**Story:** Free Chi-Tsu (Stone Godai — Dharan and Quagon)  
**Boss:** Stone angel warden → Chi-Tsu (friendly after victory)

### World 8: Factory Complex (Demon Production Facility)
**Theme:** Industrial, machines, Tempura's war machines  
**Gimmick:** Conveyor belts, crushers, laser grids  
**Story:** Sabotage Tempura's factory, confront demon elite  
**Boss:** Mecha commander

### World 9: Tempura's Fortress
**Theme:** Dark castle, void energy, final challenge  
**Gimmick:** All mechanics combined  
**Story:** Final confrontation with Lord Tempura  
**Boss:** Lord Tempura (multi-phase final boss)

## 6.8 WORLD LORE

### Gemcraft System

The magic system of Yaqar.

**Gem Types:**
- **Fire Gems** — Flame attacks, Burning Bush transformation
- **Ice Gems** — Freeze, snow, Phocid transformation
- **Rock Gems** — Stone armor, mineral growth, Metalsaur transformation
- **Wind Gems** — Flight, gusts, Serpent transformation
- **Metal Gems** — Weapon creation, armor
- **Mind Gems** — Telepathy, illusions, control
- **Life Gems** — Healing, creation
- **Death Gems** — Harm, undead
- **Space Gems** — Teleportation, size alteration
- **Time Gems** — Temporal manipulation

### Angelcraft vs. Demoncraft

- **Angelcraft:** An Angel descends, performs a Miracle, then leaves
- **Demoncraft (Witchcraft):** A Demon possesses a willing host to cast Curses
- **Lien's Advantage:** As half-Angel, she can use Angelcraft directly

### The Church and Dragons

- **Apostolic Church:** Main human religion, suspicious of Dragons and unsanctioned Gemcraft
- **Anigi's Dragon Church:** Serves Dragons, teaches them to help humans reach Paradise
- **Tension:** Human authorities distrust Anigi for associating with Dragons

### Species of Yaqar

- **Humans:** Multiple cultures (Furiga, Ispani, Moztoc, Lemp, etc.)
- **Dragons:** Many species (Balloon, Fire, Ice, Stone, Wyvern, etc.)
- **Demons:** Seven classes based on Deadly Sins
- **Angels:** Nine choirs (Guardian through Seraph)
- **Godai:** Five elemental serpent deities
- **Others:** Grouches, Slimes, Shroomen, Duendes, etc.
